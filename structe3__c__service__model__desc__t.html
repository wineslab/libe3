Detailed Description

Descriptor describing a ServiceModel implemented in C.

  • name, version and ran_function_id identify the SM.
  • telemetry_ids and control_ids point to arrays that will be copied by the implementation; the caller may free them after creating the SM.
  • Callbacks are invoked by the C++ adapter and receive user_data.
