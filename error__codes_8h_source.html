29/**
30 * @brief X-macro list of all libe3 error codes.

32 * Expand with `#define X(name, val) ...` before using this macro,
33 * and `#undef X` afterwards. Each entry exposes a symbolic name and its
34 * corresponding integer value.

41 * | ALREADY_INITIALIZED | -3 | Component is already initialized |
42 * | NOT_CONNECTED | -4 | No active connection available |
43 * | CONNECTION_FAILED | -5 | Failed to establish a connection |
44 * | TIMEOUT | -6 | Operation timed out |
45 * | ENCODE_FAILED | -7 | PDU encoding failed |
46 * | DECODE_FAILED | -8 | PDU decoding failed |
47 * | SM_NOT_FOUND | -9 | Requested Service Model was not found |
48 * | SM_ALREADY_REGISTERED | -10 | Service Model is already registered |
49 * | BUFFER_TOO_SMALL | -11 | Provided buffer is too small |
50 * | INTERNAL_ERROR | -12 | Unexpected internal error |
51 * | SUBSCRIPTION_EXISTS | -13 | Subscription already exists |
